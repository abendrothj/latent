# Welcome to Latent

**Latent** is your local-first AI research assistant. It combines the simplicity of Markdown notes with the intelligence of AI agents.

## Features

- **Markdown Editing**: Write in plain text, full control
- **[[ai-assistant|AI Assistant]]**: Context-aware AI that knows your notes
- **[[semantic-search|Semantic Search]]**: Find notes by meaning, not just keywords
- **[[backlinks|Backlinks]]**: Automatic bidirectional linking
- **Privacy First**: All data stays on your machine

## Getting Started

1. Start writing notes in Markdown
2. Use `[[note-name]]` to link between notes
3. Ask the AI assistant questions about your notes
4. Let the system index and connect your knowledge

## Example Questions

Try asking the assistant:

- "What are my notes about?"
- "Search for quantum computing"
- "Create a new note summarizing my research on AI"
- "What notes link to this one?"
